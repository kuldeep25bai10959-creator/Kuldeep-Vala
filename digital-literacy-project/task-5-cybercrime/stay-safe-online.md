Stay Safe Online Checklist



Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.



Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible.



Do not click suspicious links or unknown emails.



Avoid sharing personal information on public websites.



Always check website URLs before entering sensitive data.



Keep your software and antivirus updated.



Use secure Wi-Fi networks and avoid public Wi-Fi for banking.



Log out from accounts when using shared computers.



Regularly monitor your bank accounts for unusual activity.
